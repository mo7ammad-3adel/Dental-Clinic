Not insured? No problem!

We are primarily an out-of-network provider — also known as a “fee-for-service” dentist. Working outside of the insurance network allows us to charge a fair and fixed rate for procedures rather than have fees dictated by a third-party insurance provider.

Being a fee-for-service dental office allows us to recommend more conservative treatment options. Our philosophy is that the less dental treatment we can do for you to keep you healthy, the better off you are!

Questions about the estimated cost of treatment or paying for your care? Don’t hesitate to reach out.

Insurance

As a courtesy to our patients, we will collect your estimated co-payment at the time of service, then file a claim to your insurance company on your behalf. If there is any balance remaining, we’ll contact you for final payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose a fee-for-service dentist?

Being a fee-for-service practice is a benefit to our patients. Dental offices that work as a “preferred provider” with an insurance company agree to a 10-45% fee reduction for every procedure they perform. That means the dentist must do more procedures to make up for the fee difference. Unfortunately, this often leads to a dentist over-recommending treatments that you don’t necessarily need.

How much will it cost?

Our team will always provide you with an estimated cost before your treatment. At that time, we can also discuss your payment options, based on your situation. For those with dental insurance, we don’t always know in advance what services (if any) your insurance company will cover. The insurance company provides estimates on procedures they will cover based on your plan. We’ll work with you to make sure you feel comfortable with this estimation.
