IT TAKES A VILLAGE – HELP US GIVE THE GIFT OF A BRAND NEW SMILE

Village Dental Gives Back

Nominate someone to win a free smile makeover!

At Village Dental, we understand that not everyone has access to the dental care they need to maintain a healthy, radiant smile. That’s why we’re excited to introduce our community outreach program — It Takes a Village.

We’ll be offering complimentary dental care to well-deserving individuals who merit a brighter future. This smile makeover will be completely personalized to meet their goals and dental health needs.

How does it work?

Do you know someone who could benefit from this extraordinary opportunity? Simply nominate them below! Complete the form, sharing their story and explaining why they could benefit from this complimentary opportunity. We’ll take it from there!

Our commitment

We’ll select 2 winners every quarter. These individuals will be chosen based on their circumstances and the impact that receiving dental care could have on their lives. Whether it’s improving their overall health or simply giving them a reason to smile again, we want to hear their stories!

What winners can expect

Selected winners will receive a smile makeover tailored to their needs and goals. Our team will work diligently to provide comprehensive dental care, ensuring a radiant and healthy smile.

One-time smile makeover treatment

This opportunity is a one-time smile makeover treatment. We believe that by offering this transformative experience, we can create a lasting impact on the lives of our community members.

Who can participate?

Anyone with the wish of having a bright smile. Whether they’re a neighbor down the street or a member of our extended community, we welcome nominations from anyone who knows someone in need of dental care.

Nominate Someone Today

At Village Dental, we believe that everyone deserves a healthy, radiant smile. Through It Takes a Village, we hope to make that a reality for those who need it most.

Thank you for your support in making a difference in our community. Together, we can create brighter, healthier smiles for all. Have questions? Give us a call at (720) 730-9140.
