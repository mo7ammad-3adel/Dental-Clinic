Frequently Asked Questions

More questions? We’re ready to help. Give us a call at (720) 730-9140 or book your free consultation now.

When do you start seeing kids?

We offer a “Happy Visit” for kids starting at age 3. This is a 30-minute visit where we’ll help your child feel comfortable at the dentist and take a look at their teeth.

Is a cleaning included in my first visit?

Yes! Your first visit to our office will include a dental cleaning, exam, and 3D x-rays. We’ll recommend follow-up treatments as needed.

How much will treatment cost?

We always recommend meeting with the doctors to discuss your dental concerns and any costs that may be associated with necessary treatment. Your dentists will give you a complete treatment plan with options for you to consider. Then you can make a choice of what option best fits your budget and how you want to proceed. We offer numerous financing options to help make your decision easy.

Do you do cosmetic dentistry?

Yes! Virtually any cosmetic concern can be treated here in our office.

Do you do tooth-colored fillings?

Yes. We offer white cavity fillings to restore your smile in a way that seamlessly matches the rest of your teeth.

Do you do implants?

We do. Our dentists have placed thousands of long-lasting dental implants with great results.

Do you offer In-office whitening?

Yes! We offer professional teeth whitening here in our dental spa. Sit back, relax, and catch up on your favorite show while we brighten your smile a shade or two.
