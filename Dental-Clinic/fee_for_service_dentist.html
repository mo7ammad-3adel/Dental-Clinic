A dental care experience that prioritizes you

At Village Dentistry, we believe that dental care is about more than just healthy teeth and gums; it’s about a relationship built on trust, comfort, and understanding. Our practice uses a fee-for-service model — an approach that ensures your dental health, preferences, and satisfaction come first.

As a fee-for-service practice, we’re driven by the desire to provide personalized, high-quality dental care that meets your unique needs. We’re not bound by the restrictions of insurance-based practices, and this freedom allows us to focus on what truly matters — delivering the best possible dental care for you.

What is fee-for-service dentistry?

Fee-for-service dentistry is a payment model for dental care where the dental office bills insurance for the usual and customary fees of the treatment provided. This model contrasts with insurance-based practices, where the services covered and their costs are often predetermined by the patient’s dental insurance policy.

Under the fee-for-service model, you and our dentists have more autonomy over the treatment plan. Our dentists can propose a wide range of treatments without being limited by what an insurance policy will cover. You, in turn, are free to choose from these options based on your specific dental health needs and preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

More questions? We’re ready to help. Give us a call at (720) 730-9140.

How is fee-for-service dentistry different from insurance-based practices?

With the fee-for-service model, you and your dentist have more autonomy in deciding the treatment plan. Our dentists can recommend a broader range of treatments without being limited by what an insurance policy will cover, and you can choose from these options based on your needs and preferences.

Can I still submit claims to my insurance provider for treatment?

Yes. As a courtesy to our patients, we’ll take your estimated co-payment and submit a claim to your insurance provider. We’ll contact you if there’s still a balance to be paid.

How do I know if fee-for-service dentistry is right for me?

Choosing a dental care model is a personal decision that depends on your needs, preferences, and financial situation. Choosing a non-contracted provider like Village Dental is a good fit if you value personalized, high-quality care, more control over your treatment options, and a preventive approach to dental health.
