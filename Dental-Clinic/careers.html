At Village Dental, we’re all about providing the highest standard of general, cosmetic, and surgical dental care to our patients in a comfortable, friendly environment. Be part of our mission!

We value integrity, transparency, and service to the community, and that’s how we approach our practice. We’re dedicated to caring for patients from all walks of life and providing them with results-driven dental services.

For many, dental offices can be intimidating and even anxiety-provoking places. We’re here to change that. When you become a team member of Village Dental, you’ll work side-by-side with us to offer compassionate, first-rate care every single day. We can ensure that you’ll walk into a workplace with people that care about you and your professional development and goals.

Our team is made up of dedicated dental care providers with years of experience and unmatched expertise. When you work at Village Dental, you’ll learn from these amazing professionals as you develop your skills and approach.

Office Culture

At Village Dental, our employee culture is our most valuable asset. The collective sum of the individual differences, life experiences, knowledge, inventiveness, innovation, self-expression, unique capabilities, and talent that our employees invest in their work represents a significant part of our culture, reputation, and our company’s achievement.

We believe Village Dental succeeds when our employees feel valued, have equitable access to opportunities for personal development, and feel a sense of daily accomplishment. We foster these goals through an atmosphere of fun, inclusion, open communication, and teamwork. Through these efforts, we turn our energy outward to positively impact our patient’s experience by creating an open, transparent, and positive environment.
